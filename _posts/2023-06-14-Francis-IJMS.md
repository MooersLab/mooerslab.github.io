Francis's dissertation work on targeting RNA structure in drug design was [published](https://www.mdpi.com/1422-0067/24/12/10110) in the International Journal of Molecular Sciences. He did virutal screening of a vast library on a super computer, machine learning of the leads, molecular dynamics simulations of the lead complexes, and in vitro testing of the binding with microscale thermophoresis. <br />
