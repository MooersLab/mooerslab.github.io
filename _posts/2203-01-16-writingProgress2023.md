A lot of books on writing tout the value of tracking writing progress.
None of them address to my satisfaction how to cope with the problem of juggling mulitple writing projects in parallel.
This is a common dilema that many academics face.
My approach is to track writing progress on a per project basis in an Excel workbook that automatically tallies the progress for the day by word count and minutes spent and then automatically updates the a bar graph of the cumulative progess on a montly basis.
The entry of the progress per project requires only a few seconds.
I include a webpage where you can paste text into a text area to get the word count.
I have update the [workbook for 2023](https://github.com/MooersLab/writingProgess2023), and I have updated the extensive README.md file.
