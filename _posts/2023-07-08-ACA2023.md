In the session on Crystal Growth at the [2023 meeting](https://www.acameeting.com/) of the American Crystallographic Association in Baltimore, I presented Syed Aslam's results of applying a Definitive Screening Design to the problem of growing larger RNA crystals. This work builds on the work by Barat Venkataramany and many other lab members. This talk generated a lot of excitement. The slides and sample spreadsheet are located [here](https://github.com/MooersLab/dsd4xtals). <br />
