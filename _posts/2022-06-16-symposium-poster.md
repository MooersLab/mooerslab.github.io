I presented Francis's dissertation work on a poster at the [*10th Annual Oklahoma Structural Biology Symposium*](https://www.ou.edu/structuralbiology/events) at the University of Oklahoma.
The symposium was held at the National Storm Prediction Center.
This was the final symposium in this series. 
The symposium was funded by the Oklahoma COBRE in Structural Biology, which is led by Dr. Ann West. 
It was attended by well over 100 people from Oklahoma and around the country.
The symposium series ended on a high note.

Phase III of this COBRE was just funded.
