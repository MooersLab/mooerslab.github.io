On September 29th at the 2022 SSRL/LCLS Users Meeting, Mooers was awarded the [Ferrel Lytle Award](https://www6.slac.stanford.edu/news/2022-10-26-blaine-mooers-wins-2022-lytle-award-decades-synchrotron-leadership-and-rna-research) for his service on the SSRL User executive committee and his research using SSRL.
