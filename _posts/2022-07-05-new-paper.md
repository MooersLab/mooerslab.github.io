Our second collaborative paper with the Razi Hussaini Lab of the Department of Chemistry, U of Tulsa, was published on-line in the journal [**Royal Society of Chemistry Advances**](https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2022/ra/d2ra02415b).
Francis did a series of docking experiments and molecular dynamics simulations with the structure of the nicotinic acetyl-choline receptor α6-nAchR embedded in a bilayer for this study. 
This work was done towards the goal of developing better smoking cessation drugs.
